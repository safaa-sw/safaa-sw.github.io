  • Event Invitation App

    website to organize an invitation to an event; The site provides the possibility for the user to register to attend the event. It allows the event organizer to send invitations to attend the event and accept attendance requests registered by users; The event organizer can also specify the seating places for the invitees in the hall where the event will be held. In addition to obtaining reports regarding the invitees, empty seats, invitees who attended, invitees who were absent, and other reports.
