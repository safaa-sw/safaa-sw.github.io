  • Administrative Communications App

    The administrative communications system provides the ability to deal with transactions of various types, incoming and outgoing. The system administrator can deal with employee and department data and determine the levels of confidentiality and importance, the types of transactions that can be handled, and other main menus in the system. It is also possible to follow the status of the transaction and review the operations that were performed on it from its inception until its termination. The employee can, according to his privileges, create a new transaction, review the transactions in his mail, and perform a set of operations on them, such as adding an attachment, transferring them to another employee, or closing them.
    php, laravel, mysql, bootstrap, jquery, vs code.
